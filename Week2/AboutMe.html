About Me

Anthony Kazyaka is studying Computer Science at Michigan State University. He has tried to make games since he was a kid, and has recently broadened to application development as well. He has had internship opportunities with Vectorform LLC and Quicken Loans, working with C# in a variety of applications.
Anthony is most knowledgeable about programming using C# in the .NET framework, but also knows several other languages and frameworks, including: HTML, JavaScript, jQuery, C++, XNA, WPF, Unity3D scripting, and SQL. He prides himself on both his programming knowledge base and his problem solving skills, both of which he uses to tackle a wide range of tasks.
In his spare time, Anthony can be found playing Xbox or spending time with his friends. If you want to learn more, or contact him, he can be reached at ask.anthony@hotmail.com, or follow him on Twitter: AnthonyKazyaka.

What I find most annoying about the site is that the pop-up boxes that would cause them to pop-up and collapse rapidly, and you could get two of them that were next to each other to pop-up and collapse alternatively.
