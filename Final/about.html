About CrowdSource

About the app

CrowdSource is an app that was made by Steam Hatch Studios to answer life's most important questions like:

  • What is the meaning of life?
  • How do you properly pronounce "comic sans"?
  • What is the best pizza topping?
  • Which console is king?

and many more. Can't think of something to ask right now? Answer someone else's question to help them uncover the universe's great secrets, which type of mustard most adults prefer, or what type of books kids like the most.

About us at Steam Hatch

Steam Hatch Studios, the team behind CrowdSource, is a group of four friends who have a passion for making quality applications and games. Anthony, Sam, and Alex currently attend Michigan State University, while Justin currently attends Michigan Tech.
