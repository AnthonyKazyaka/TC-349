Morph: A game created by my team in five weeks this semester.

The game is a Sonic-inspired coin collection game where you race against the clock to get the highest score and place top on the leaderboards!

At your disposal are four different shapes, each of which have different abilities! Use them to your advantage to roll, dash, jump, and glide your way to 1st place!
